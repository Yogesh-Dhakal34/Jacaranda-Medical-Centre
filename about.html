Jacaranda Blue-Mist Medical Centre was founded with a single vision — to make world-class healthcare accessible to every person, regardless of where they live. From the heart of the city to the most remote corners of Nepal, we are committed to delivering compassionate, professional medical care.

Our team of certified specialists, dedicated nurses, and support staff work around the clock to ensure every patient feels safe, heard, and cared for. We combine modern medical technology with a human-first approach that puts your wellbeing above all else.
